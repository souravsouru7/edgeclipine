Chapter 02 · The Journey

Two years.
Countless setups.
Same mistakes.

Year 1 · Early Days

The Honeymoon Phase

Got into trading full of fire. Watched YouTube for weeks. Learned RSI, MACD, support-resistance. Hit a few trades right early on. Felt like I had discovered a superpower. Doubled my position size. Then the market humbled me, fast.

"I had 5 green trades in a row. I thought I cracked it. The market was just being polite."
Year 1 · Mid Phase

The Strategy Trap

Every loss led to a new strategy. Tried Wyckoff. Then ICT. Then scalping. Then swing. Bought 3 courses. Joined 2 Discord servers. The problem was never the strategy. I just couldn't see it yet.

"I was solving the wrong problem, with the wrong tools, every single time."
Year 1 · Late Phase

The Spiral

Revenge trading on bad days. Exiting winners early out of fear. Letting losers run because hope feels like a plan. I knew all of this was wrong while I was doing it. Knowing isn't enough. You need to see the pattern in cold data.

"Thursday afternoons were destroying me. I had no idea until months later."
Year 2 · The Shift

One Spreadsheet. Everything Changed.

Started logging every trade manually. Entry, exit, emotion, setup, result. Painful. Three weeks in, I looked at the data and felt sick — in a good way. The patterns were obvious. I just needed a mirror. My best setups. My worst time of day. My real win rate, not the imaginary one.

"The data didn't lie. I just hadn't been looking at it."
